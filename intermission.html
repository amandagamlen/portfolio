Intermission

Blender, Premiere Pro
(2023)
Motion

Intermission explores the concept of liminality through 3D animation, taking viewers on an immersive first-person journey, to a once-beloved, now-abandoned video store. As the journey progresses, the environment shifts into a series of surreal pool rooms, symbolising the fluid and often disjointed nature of nostalgia.
