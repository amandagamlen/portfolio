Maramataka

Interaction
(2022)
figma

This project was dedicated to designing an app that honours the rich tradition of lunar observation, particularly within Māori culture. Through the lens of the maramataka, the Māori lunar calendar, the app provides users with an interactive platform to engage with the lunar phases and their cultural significance.

The goal was to bridge ancient wisdom with modern technology, foster understanding, and encourage users to explore Māori teachings further. Through the app, users have the opportunity to reconnect with nature and indigenous knowledge, promoting holistic well-being and cultural preservation.
