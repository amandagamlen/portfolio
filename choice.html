CHOICE

web design, visual identity
(2022)


Throughout history, women have fought for autonomy over their bodies, including the right to make decisions about their reproductive health. In Aotearoa, this struggle has taken on various forms, shaped by cultural, social, and political factors.

This project aims to educate and raise awareness about the importance of reproductive rights, within the context of New Zealand's history.
